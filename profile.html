03 / Cover letter

A note to hiring teams.

Open to the right opportunity · Berlin, Germany

Dear Hiring Team,

I'm a Technical Program Manager with 15+ years across Microsoft, Amazon, Philips, and DoorDash·Wolt, and a track record of turning complex, ambiguous problems into shipped systems. Most recently at Wolt, I led the post-merger program unifying DoorDash and Wolt platforms and put policies in place that stopped SMS-pumping fraud — saving €600K.

At Amazon, I spent eight years as a Principal TPM building the machinery of fulfilment: launching scanning technology across EU sort centers, externalizing Amazon's Warehouse Management System for third-party partners, and driving standardization across seven engineering teams in three timezones. I started as an engineer at Microsoft, and that foundation still shapes how I work — I stay close to the architecture, the data, and the trade-offs.

What sets me apart is that I work across every discipline it takes to ship: I write code as a developer, define products as a product manager, and run delivery as a program and project manager. My indie studio, Vuolt, is proof — seven apps on the App Store, each one designed, coded, and launched solo. I bring an engineer's rigour, an MBA's commercial lens, and a maker's bias for shipping.

I'd welcome a conversation about how I can help your team deliver programs that matter.
